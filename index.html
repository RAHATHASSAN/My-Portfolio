About Me

Hello! I’m Md Sazid Hassan Rahat, a passionate Frontend Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Digital Marketing enthusiast with over 1+ year of experience in this field. I specialize in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly web applications with a strong focus on clean design and smooth user experience.

In digital marketing, I have hands-on experience running Facebook ad campaigns, managing email marketing, and developing social media strategies to improve brand awareness and engagement. Currently, I am working on a fashion house advertising campaign where I handle creative design, ad strategy, and campaign execution.

I have completed and worked on multiple projects in web development, UI/UX design, and digital marketing, which have strengthened both my technical and creative expertise.

Currently, I am gaining real-world industry experience through an internship at Shahjalal Islami Bank PLC, where I am working in the IT Security department using open-source tools. This experience is helping me understand enterprise-level systems, security practices, and real-world IT operations.

With over a year of experience, I am continuously learning, improving my skills, and working on impactful projects that combine creativity, technology, and real-world problem-solving.
